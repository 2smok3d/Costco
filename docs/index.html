Complete AS400 Command Reference

CONFIRMED — AS400 Guide Feb 2026
IAI
Item Alpha Inquiry
Gateway to ~80% of merchandise data. Search by description, item#, dept, or UPC. Enter partial descriptions to get a list.
Opt 1 = on-hand by warehouse (overnight data — warn members)
Opt 3 = IIL: sales by region or company
Opt 4 = RHI: receiving history
Opt 5 = Sign inquiry (no print)
Opt 6 = Print sign
Opt 8 = ITMW: your warehouse detail (most-used drill)
Opt 0 = STOP: HAZMAT/disposal info
Set Display On Hand = N to see inactive items
ITMW
Item Inquiry for Warehouse
Your warehouse only. Shows on-hand, dept#, sell price, on-order, ETA, NSI, and Today's Sales in real time (updated ~10 min).
F9 = associated rebates
F14 = taxes on item
F21 = print sign from this screen
F22 = EBT eligibility + fees
Faster than IAI when you know the item#
IIL
Multi-Warehouse Inventory
On-hand across multiple warehouses. Default = your region. Use *MW, *NW, *USALL etc. Also shows sales rankings between warehouses.
CRITICAL: refreshes OVERNIGHT only — never promise real-time
Used to redirect members or find stock for transfers
Use *USALL for the broadest search
IGM
Item Group Maintenance
Create and maintain item groups for tracking sets of SKUs together (e.g., your top-10 meat items, CMN donations, etc.).
F23 = delete the group
Groups must be active items in your warehouse
Used with ISI and COMP for comparison analysis
ISI
Item Sales Inquiry
Item sales for a single item or a group (created in IGM). View specific item sales patterns or compare item groups.
Enter item numbers directly OR select a saved group
Key report for planning cut lists in meat
IRCI
Instant Rebate Coupon Inquiry
View active, expired, and FUTURE IRC coupons. Shows item#, discount amount, start/end dates, manufacturer vs. Costco funded, qty limit.
Drop the last (check) digit when entering coupon# — Costco convention
Mark Active, Expired, or Future with (x)
Enter start date to view expired IRCs
Both start AND end date needed for future IRCs
IXI
Item ↔ UPC Cross-Reference
Cross-reference an item number with all associated UPCs. Use when item won't scan at the register to verify if the UPC is set up.
If UPC is set up but not scanning: do an EPC download
RF Scanner Option 88 does the same thing handheld
RHI
Receiving History Inquiry
Every PO/delivery received for an item: date, vendor, PO#, quantity, cost. Critical for receiving errors and variance investigation.
Run same-day after every meat delivery — catch shorts 5x easier
Cross-reference against physical pallet count
Accessible via Opt 4 from IAI
DSI
Daily Sales / Inventory Inquiry
Department sales and inventory for your warehouse, region, or company — daily, weekly, period, and YTD. The morning scoreboard. NOT same-day.
View by warehouse = all depts for selected date
View by dept = all warehouses vs. your location
Start every shift here — know your numbers before your manager does
DFI
Daily Flash Sales Inquiry
Daily, weekly, period-to-date and YTD flash sales by warehouse. Similar to DSI. Not same-day — pulls next day.
Use F prompts at bottom for additional detail views
Pair with DSI — one gives totals, one gives flash breakdown
IAWD
Warehouse Dashboard
Summarized inventory, membership, sales, and front-end productivity for warehouse/region/company. Multiple views with View code field.
View 00 = main warehouse stats (default)
View 01 = dept-level on-hand + receivings + D&D vs LY
View 02 = sales by dept, comp %, penetration
View 03 = Front End productivity (simplified IAFEP)
No same-day data — previous day only
IATOP
Top Items by Department
Ranks SKUs by sales $, units, refunds, D&D, or markdowns for a chosen period. Prep tool for buyer/regional walk-throughs.
Leave Dept blank = top items for entire warehouse
F19/F20 = toggle comparison between warehouses or regions
Filter by category code to narrow (e.g. ribeye only)
Pair with IIL: if you're #1 in sales but low on-hand → incoming OOS
Top D&D view shows your worst shrink items
STK
Sales Tracking Inquiry
Item sales AND D&D in units for a 4-week period by dept. Most used in fresh depts for production planning. THE daily production reference.
F19/F20 = toggle left/right through data
F21 = print full report
F22 = export to Excel — build your own production sheet
Key input for your daily cut list decisions
IAD
Item Activity by Department
On-hand adjustments or destroyed items in dollars for a selected dept, fiscal year, period, and week. Extended cost and sell amounts per item.
Shows net landing cost + sell cost per shrink item
Use to build your shrink-dollar case for reducing over-production
IAIMI
Inventory Movement Summary
Stock status summary by company, region, warehouse, and dept. Also shows UPC activity — how many times an item was hand-keyed vs. scanned.
F15 = Sales/UPC view: see hand-key vs. scan ratio per item
High hand-key rate on a meat item = label issue or UPC problem
ISO
Item Sales by Operator ID
Sales by warehouse and item number broken down by individual cashier/clerk operator. Total units + dollars per operator for a date range.
Used for front-end audits and training reviews
Can spot if a specific item is being dept-ringed instead of scanned
COMP
Comparison Analysis
Review sales history by item, group, dept, vendor, location, or date range for the last 3 fiscal years. Build your year-over-year story here.
Works with item groups created in IGM
3-year view = the most powerful trend tool at your level
DASH
Receiving Dashboard
All inbound trucks and appointments for the day: PO#, vendor, ETA, status, door assignment. Receiving lives on this screen.
Cross-reference with RHI for a stocked-out SKU
Check before assuming a product won't arrive today
More detail in the full Receiving Manual
EDSR
Electronic Data Sign Request
Print a price sign when you know the item# and need no manual changes. Select size (M/L are most common in the warehouse).
Shift+F9 (F21) = print; select printer 1 (main) or 2 (mobile)
Walk the floor with EDSR printout before opening daily
Can also enter item UPC instead of item#
Accessible via Opt 6 from IAI
EDMP
Print Daily Maintenance
All buyer-side sign/price maintenance affecting signs for the day. Updated at 3:00 AM local time — do not run before then.
Filter: on-hand items only OR items with price changes
Signs MUST be printed and hung before opening every day
Change date for holiday pre-staging
Default sign size M (medium)
EDSM
Sign Maintenance (Custom)
Customize signs manually. Use for "last one display," immediate price/description corrections before overnight buyer updates.
Leave item# blank = Free Form blank sign
Signs print AFTER you exit (F3) — not immediately
Note: unit price (per oz etc.) doesn't auto-update with price change
EPC
Emergency Price Change
Mark down items or correct prices at the warehouse level for the day. Requires WPM password. Access typically limited to staff managers.
F6 = add new price change; enter item# and new price + reason
Copy prior-day EPC: change date, place 3 next to EPC to copy
F15 = Item Downloads (after EPC, to restore original price)
F18 = Blocked Items (K = key flick, P = prohibit sale)
This is your markdown tool for end-of-life meat
EPCI
EPC Inquiry (Read-Only)
View-only: shows all EPCs, item downloads, and blocked items entered in the system. No password required to view.
STK
Sales Tracking — Meat Focus
Your primary cut-planning tool. 4-week unit sales + D&D by item for your dept. F22 exports to Excel for custom production sheets.
Run every morning before building your cut list
Dept = your meat dept# (usually Dept 17 cooler + related)
F22 export → build your own yield/cover tracking spreadsheet
MACR
Merchandise Audit Count List
Full dept inventory listing: every item with on-hand and NSI (non-saleable inventory). Used for cycle counts and pre-buyer visit prep.
Add category code to filter (e.g. ribeye items only)
Print before every physical inventory count
RF Scanner Option 22 prints same report handheld
IATOP
Top Items — Meat D&D View
Filter to your meat dept and select D&D sort — shows your highest-shrink items. If the same SKU keeps showing up week after week, it's a production problem.
Run weekly. If ribeye keeps hitting top 5 D&D, you're over-cutting
Bring IATOP D&D + STK sales data to show the fix = credibility
LSDT
Daily Labor Tracking
Near real-time view of today's sales vs. plan PLUS hours scheduled vs. used. Supervisor's decision tool for calling people in or sending them home.
Available to all AS400 sign-ons — home warehouse only
View in 15/30/60 min intervals
Actl Lane vs. Sched Lane = are you running hot or cold?
Sls Diff = cumulative actual vs. plan — your real-time gap
LSDC
Door Count Entry
Enter door counts throughout the day so supervisors know how many lanes to open. Manual entry at defined intervals.
LSIM
Sales Impact Entry
Log unusual sales impacts (large resale orders, big jewelry purchases, large returns). F6 to add — requires reason + dollar amount.
These feed into NEXT year's sales planning — affects scheduling forecasts
Resale, refunds, kiosks, gas, jewelry auto-populate without manual entry
LSCM
Labor Schedule Control File
Set dollars per labor hour, register count, and opening/closing hours. Drives schedule recommendations. Staff managers+ access.
Update for holiday hours to get accurate schedule suggestions
LSWM
Weekly Sales Plan Entry
Enter projected sales — feeds LSDT planned figures and drives scheduled hours suggestions. GMs, AGMs, FRNs, ADMs access only.
MBA9
Membership Lookup (View-Only)
View-only membership lookup. Search by: membership#, last/first name, sounds-like, ID type, 7-digit phone, or mailing address. Toggle with F11.
F11 = toggle search method
F4 = action code prompts (on found member)
Safer for routine verification — cannot edit
WII
Warehouse Item Sales Inquiry
Member purchase history + transactions. The refund desk tool. Requires membership# PLUS item# or dept# (not both at once).
F11 = toggle to show item descriptions (not just item#)
Refunds show as negative Ext Sell price
White highlighted items = had an IRC when purchased
CRITICAL: does NOT include Costco.com orders (use EOI)
10-year history limit per privacy laws
F21 = print transaction for member
MRC
Membership Report Card
Membership dashboard: income vs. LY and plan, new sign-ups, renewals, Executive upgrades. By company, region/state, or warehouse.
F11 = toggle to Captured Renewal Rate and Renewal Trend
Know your renewal rate — managers bring this to every leadership meeting
EPRFM
Employee Profile Maintenance
Set up AS400 user profiles. Assigns specialty access: Optical, Inventory Audit, RTV, Sales Audit, Vault. STAFF MANAGERS AND ABOVE ONLY.
F4 = list available position types
F6 = assign user; F8 = finalize; F23 = disable profile
For combo employee: select Sales Audit first — exposes multi-dept option
Full-time Sales Auditors cannot carry any other profile
Assigned JTS triggers Costco U training automatically
EPRFD
Employee Profile Display
Read-only view of profiles set up via EPRFM. Use to audit access levels and troubleshoot "I can't see X" issues.
WPM
Warehouse Password Maintenance
Add/change/delete users for protected programs: APR (Refund Audit), EPC, TCP (Tire), TSH (Trash Scan). AWM and WM access only.
F6 = add new user; 5–10 char password, no special characters
PWMI = view who has passwords for each option
TSH access = who can scan trash (destroy inventory)
EPC access = who can change prices day-of
RPWD
Role-Based Password Reset
Reset a role-based AS400 profile password. Staff managers+ access. Employee must set their own new password on first login after reset.
IAFEP
Front-End Productivity
Items per minute, members per hour, dollars per sign-on hour per cashier/register. Also shows Self-Checkout stats separately.
F19/F20 = toggle between Data view and Time view
Enter Y in Self-Checkout field for SCO-specific stats
F1 Help = detailed breakdown of every metric and its calculation
IAWD View 03 = simplified version of this screen
OUTQ / SOUTQ
Print Queue Management
Manage print jobs in the output queue. View, hold, release, reroute, or delete reports. Signs use SOUTQ (separate from document queues).
Opt 2 = change (reroute printer, copies, start page)
Opt 4 = delete (irreversible)
Opt 5 = view without printing
Opt 6 = release to print
F18 (Shift+F6) = jump to bottom of queue (most recent)
F19 (Shift+F7) = list other outqueues
Sign print jobs vanish after printing (unlike document jobs)
RDPRT / SPRT
Reroute / Restart Printer
Reroute sign printer from Printer 1 to Printer 2 without calling the Service Desk. SPRT restarts a printer. Reduces sign-printing downtime.
RDPRT → select source → select destination → SPRT to restart
Reset option (Opt 6) only undoes the LAST reroute
RLG
Member Refund Log
View refund transaction info with multiple filters. Defaults to home warehouse, previous day. Y = dept ring refund. * = dept ring converted to inline item.
Enter X next to operator# to see specific return details
Tracking# column not visible same day
SAST
Transaction Audit Search
Find multiple transactions on a specific register for a date range. Filter by transaction type, tender type. Multiple filters simultaneously.
Items grouped by dept and item# ascending — not scan order
For exact scan sequence: use the EDP controller
TAB to transaction → Field ENTER to drill in
CDCSI
Cost Dept Category Sales Inquiry
View cost department margin components by location, dept, and category. Primarily shows Initial Markup % (IMU) for specific items.
IMU = your department's gross margin foundation per category
Key for understanding why your dept P&L looks the way it does
WWPO
Work With Purchase Orders
View open POs, standard distributions, auto-distributions, and transfer requests. 4 views: Summary, Status, Item, Freight/Budget.
Check this to verify a meat delivery PO before the truck arrives
Tire Center uses this for incoming tire POs primarily

Receiving a Meat Truck

  • Check DASH for PO#, vendor, ETA before truck arrives
  • On receipt: verify PO vs. invoice vs. actual physical count
  • Catch-weight reconciliation: scanned weight must equal invoice ±0.5%
  • Verify COOL declaration on master case matches AS400 item master Required on: lamb, goat, chicken muscle cuts. Beef/pork exempt since 2016.
  • Run RHI same-day to confirm posting — do NOT wait until next day
  • Flag any variance immediately — same-day resolution is 5× easier

Days of Supply (DOS) Formula

DOS = On-Hand ÷ Avg Daily Movement

Get On-Hand from ITMW. Get Avg Daily Movement from STK (4-week total ÷ 28). When DOS exceeds the pull-date window → markdown now (EPC), not tomorrow.

Action Rule
DOS > pull-date window → mark down immediately. Still unsold at pull date → shrink out as "Out-of-Date." Members can request freeze-before-checkout on marked-down meat.

Production Tracking — Sub-Primal to Retail Cut

Cut-in-store production is logged on a paper production sheet (cut, weight, trays, cutter initials) and keyed into AS400 at end-of-shift.

DECREASE
Sub-Primal On-Hand (bulk input)
INCREASE
Retail Cut On-Hand (saleable output)
DIFFERENCE
Trim / Fat / Bone Shrink

Yield = Saleable Cut Weight ÷ Input Sub-Primal Weight. Typical beef sub-primal: 70–85% retail cuts depending on grade and trim spec. Know your numbers — if you're consistently hitting 68%, something is wrong in your trim habits.

In-Store Cut Specifications

SOURCE: OFFICIAL COSTCO CUT SPEC PDFs
Spec Philosophy
Same thickness across the tray beats one gorgeous steak. Straight parallel cuts beat creative cutting every time in a production case. The case sells confidence — a loaded tray with clean alignment reads "good value" instantly. Consistency = career advancement here.
PRIME Boneless Beef Ribeye Steak (1pc) PLU #333 / Item #34323
  1. Tenderize TWICE the whole Boneless Ribeye. Fat side down.
  2. Trim any back strap. Remove bone chips, bone felt, or connective tissue.
  3. Trim and remove all finger meat from bottom of Ribeye. DO NOT remove rib fat.
  4. Scrape exposed ends to remove any bone dust.
  5. Cut the whole Ribeye into steaks.
  6. Trim the external fat to ¼ inch — follow the contour of the steak.
  7. Wrap and label with USDA Prime sticker.
PRIME Boneless Beef Top Sirloin – Cap Off PLU #1660 / Item #42357
  1. Tenderize TWICE the whole Boneless Sirloin. Fat side down.
  2. Remove any remaining fat, cartilage, or connective tissue down to the blue membrane.
  3. Cut Sirloin into steaks. Cut PERPENDICULAR to Sciatic Nerve.

Speed Tips — Power User Habits

  • Two sessions simultaneously: Press ESC → put 1 next to "Start New Session" → ENTER. Run IAI in one, DSL in the other without losing context.
  • Custom home screen: Enter desired option → F15 (Shift+F3) to add. F18 (Shift+F6) to toggle between default and custom. Fastest way to one-click your daily screens.
  • Type-ahead: Once you know the path, key your option + ENTER without waiting on screen redraws. The system buffers keystrokes.
  • Field Exit: Right-Ctrl (or numpad ENTER) — blanks the rest of a numeric field and tabs forward. FAR faster than space-tab when entering item numbers.
  • F4 on Option field: Shows all options your profile can access. Huge for discovering screens you didn't know existed.
  • Double-click blue items: Any blue text on screen can be double-clicked to auto-select. Enables text copy with Options → Edit → Double Click Auto Selection.
  • OUTQ management: Always check OUTQ after printing a large report. Opt 5 = view before printing. Opt 6 = release. Don't waste paper on a report you already have.
