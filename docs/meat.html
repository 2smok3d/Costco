— Operational Notes —
Variable-weight items: UPCs start with "2". Scale-printer applies label. First 6 digits = item ID.
Production yield target: 70–85% on beef sub-primals depending on grade and trim spec.
Shrink reason codes: Out-of-date · Spoilage · Trim/Fat/Bone · Damage/Leakers · Contamination · Markdown · Internal Use · Donation · Recall/Hold.
Receiving: each catch-weight case weighed or scanned individually. Total should equal invoice ±0.5%.
COOL compliance: Country-of-Origin required on imported chicken, lamb, goat muscle cuts. Prints on scale label automatically.
Days of Supply = on-hand ÷ avg daily sales. DOS > pull-date window = mark down immediately.
13 physical inventories per year (one per 4-week fiscal period). Pre-count: print IAI opt 1 for every active SKU.
Costco shrink target: 0.11–0.12% of sales company-wide. Track weekly with STK/DSL.
Blade-tenderized items require "BLADE TENDERIZED" on label (USDA FSIS). Check before packaging.
HACCP CCP: check and log cooler temps. Red zone = escalate immediately to supervisor.
Dept 61 Tempe Warehouse SW Region Cooler: 28–34°F OOS = call manager DOS target: ≤3 days
